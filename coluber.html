About Coluber

Coluber is a Portuguese heavy metal band inspired by the spirit of the New Wave of British Heavy Metal and the atmosphere of classic horror. Blending galloping riffs, melodic guitars and dark storytelling, the band draws from legends, ghost stories and eerie tales to create a sound rooted in tradition.

Influenced by the energy of early 1980s metal, Coluber embraces the raw spirit of the genre while building its own visual and sonic identity. From haunted streets to cursed castles and spectral apparitions, each song is part of a growing universe of dark tales and powerful heavy metal.

Band Members

Miguel Ferreira — vocals

Ricardo Almeida — lead guitar

Tiago Carvalho — rhythm guitar

Bruno Monteiro — bass
