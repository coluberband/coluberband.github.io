Coluber

A thunderous introduction to the band’s world, Coluber channels the spirit of classic NWOBHM with galloping rhythms, soaring guitar harmonies, and powerful vocals. From high-energy anthems to darker, atmosphere-driven moments, the album showcases a band rooted in tradition but already hinting at something deeper. Featuring the instrumental “Giza,” inspired by ancient Egyptian melodies, the record adds a cinematic and exotic edge to its classic metal foundation. This is where it begins. Turn it up and experience the rise of Coluber.
