About Coluber

Coluber is a Portuguese heavy metal band inspired by the spirit of the New Wave of British Heavy Metal and by the dark atmosphere of classic horror. Combining galloping riffs, melodic guitars and dramatic storytelling, the band draws from legends, ghost stories and eerie tales to create a sound rooted in tradition but driven by strong imagery and mood.

Formed with a passion for old-school metal, Coluber embraces the energy of the early 1980s while shaping its own identity through haunting themes, memorable choruses and a visual world steeped in mystery. From fog-covered streets to cursed mansions and spectral apparitions, every song is part of the band’s growing universe.
